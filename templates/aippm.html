All India Political Party Meet
The All India Political Parties Meet is a venture to emulate political realities by stimulating the various levels of policy and governance existing in our nation today. Participants are allotted specific political parties, which they represent. AIPPM is a forum for unrestricted political debate, discussion and deliberation, free from the legislative functions of the parliament.
